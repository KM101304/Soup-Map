# SoupMap Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 20, 2026

SoupMap collects only the information needed to authenticate users, show live activities on the map, support profile identity, and keep the community safe.

## What we collect

- Account identifiers provided through Sign in with Apple or email magic link
- Public profile data you add, including username, display name, avatar, bio, and interests
- Activity data you create or join
- Approximate or precise location only when you grant permission
- Reports and block actions used for moderation

## How we use data

- To authenticate your account
- To render live activities on the Vancouver map
- To let others understand who is hosting or joining an activity
- To improve trust and safety
- To send optional local reminders for upcoming activities you joined

## What we do not do

- We do not sell personal information.
- We do not use third-party ad tracking.
- We do not require always-on background location.

## Data sharing

- Activity titles, public host identity, tags, coordinates, and participant counts are shared with other app users so the live map can function.
- Reports and moderation data are only used internally for safety review.

## Data retention

- Profile data is retained while your account exists.
- Activities remain in the database for moderation and analytics unless deleted according to operational policy.
- Expired activities are removed from the live map after their end time.

## Your controls

- You can update your public profile at any time.
- You can block users.
- You can report activities.
- You can delete joined participation by leaving an activity.

## Contact

For support or privacy questions, provide your public support email and privacy-policy URL before App Store submission.
